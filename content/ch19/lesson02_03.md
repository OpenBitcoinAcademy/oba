## RGB in Practice

RGB supports fungible tokens (RGB20, including stablecoins), non-fungible tokens (RGB21), and programmable contracts. Tether has announced plans to issue USDT on RGB.

RGB-enabled Lightning nodes can transfer tokens at Lightning speed. Each commitment transaction includes an output anchoring the RGB state. Tokens move through payment channels without modifying the Lightning protocol. The Lnfi Network launched this integration on mainnet in 2025.

The LNP/BP Standards Association (a Swiss non-profit) maintains the RGB specifications and reference implementations. The protocol reached version 0.12 in 2025, adding zk-STARKs for improved privacy and auditability.
