## The Trust Spectrum

Bitcoin custody exists on a spectrum. Full self-custody with a hardware wallet and your own full node is the most trustless option. Fedimint requires trusting a threshold of community guardians. Cashu requires trusting a single mint. A custodial exchange requires trusting a corporation.

Each point on the spectrum trades convenience for trust. A beginner in the Global South may start with a Fedimint community bank and graduate to self-custody as their understanding grows. The goal is not to force everyone onto the same point but to provide options at every level of technical sophistication.

Both Fedimint and Cashu integrate with Lightning, allowing ecash users to send and receive payments across the broader Bitcoin network without requiring their own channels.
