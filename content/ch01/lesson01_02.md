## The Trust Problem

Every time you use a bank, you trust a third party. You trust them to hold your money, to process your payments, and to not freeze your account.

Most of the time, this works. But not always.

Banks fail. Governments seize accounts. Payment processors block transactions to certain countries. If the third party decides you cannot access your money, you cannot access your money.

Bitcoin removes the third party. Two people can send value directly to each other, without asking anyone for permission.
