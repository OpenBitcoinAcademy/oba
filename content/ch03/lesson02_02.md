## Why Run Your Own Node?

Running a full node serves four purposes.

**Verification without trust.** Your node validates every transaction you receive. You do not rely on a third party to tell you whether a payment is real.

**Privacy.** When you use someone else's node, they learn which addresses belong to your wallet. Your own node keeps that information local.

**Supporting the network.** Each full node that relays transactions and blocks makes the network more decentralized and harder to disrupt.

**Development access.** A full node provides a programmable API for querying blockchain data, building applications, and testing transactions.
