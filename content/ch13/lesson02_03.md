## Survivability

Security includes planning for death, incapacity, or loss of access. If you are the only person who knows your seed phrase and you become incapacitated, your bitcoin is inaccessible to your heirs.

Create an inheritance plan. Store seed phrase backups where a trusted heir can access them, but with enough security that casual discovery is unlikely. Some users use a lawyer, a safe deposit box, or a time-locked multisig arrangement.

Test your recovery plan. Restore a wallet from the seed phrase on a separate device. Verify that the funds appear. A backup that has never been tested is a backup that might not work.
