## Bitcoin Security is Different

Securing bitcoin is not like securing a bank account. There is no institution to call if something goes wrong. Possession of private keys is possession of the funds. Lose the keys and the bitcoin is gone. Have them stolen and the thief controls the funds.

Bitcoin is closer to digital cash than to a bank balance. Cash can be lost, stolen, or destroyed. So can bitcoin. The difference: bitcoin can be backed up. A wallet's keys can be copied, stored in multiple locations, and recovered from a seed phrase. Cash cannot.

This combination of risk and recoverability requires a new way of thinking about security. The user bears full responsibility.
