## Diversifying Risk

Do not store all bitcoin in a single wallet. Separate funds by purpose: a hot wallet (software, internet-connected) for daily spending with small amounts, a cold wallet (hardware, offline) for savings.

Multisignature wallets require multiple keys to authorize a transaction (2-of-3, 3-of-5). Distribute the keys across devices and locations. If one key is compromised or lost, the funds remain safe. Multisig adds complexity but removes single points of failure.

For organizations, multisig with governance policies (who holds which keys, what approval process is required) provides accountability and security beyond what any single person can achieve.
